Accuracy Of Our GPS Tracker

Standard GPS: Typically accurate within 5-10 meters in open spaces.

Enhanced GPS (with technologies like A-GPS or GLONASS): Can achieve 1-3 meter accuracy under optimal conditions.

Factors Affecting GPS Accuracy: Buildings, trees, weather conditions, and signal interference can slightly reduce accuracy.

Heart Rate Monitors: Optical heart rate sensors, commonly used in wearables, have an accuracy range of 90-95% when worn correctly and used in low-motion environments.
